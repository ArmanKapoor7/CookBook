Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Ghee

  • 1 Tbsp Oil

  • 1 Cinnamon Stick

  • 5 Black Pepper Corns

  • 3 Cloves

  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste

  • 2 Onions, chopped

  • 2 Green Chillies, chopped

  • 250 Gram Mutton Keema

  • 4 Sticks Mint leaves, chopped

  • 2 tsp Salt

  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric

  • 3 tsp Meat Masala

  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder

  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder

  • 1 Tomato, chopped

  • 1/2 Bunch Coriander Leaves, chopped

  • 1 Cup Cooked Rice

How to Make

1.Take a pan, put some ghee and oil.

2.Add cinnamon stick, black pepper corn and clove. Mix a little.

3.Add ginger garlic paste, chopped onions and green chillies. Saute them.

4.Now add mutton keema, chopped mint leaves and salt. Mix them well for some time on high flame.

5.Put turmeric, meat masala, red chilli powder, cumin powder. Mix a little and add chopped tomato along with chopped mint leaves and chopped coriander leaves.

6.Thoroughly mix them and then add cooked rice to the mixture.

7.Stir the mixture with the rice thoroughly.

8.Serve the Keema Pulav hot.
